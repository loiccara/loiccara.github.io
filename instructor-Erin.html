Meet your Instructors

"Dancing is a life-long learning journey, it's so important to take as many lessons from as many different teachers as possible and always go back to your basics'.

Erin, the Promocode Queen

Erin has been dancing salsa (and yes, bachata and Kizomba too) since 2010 however fell in love with Salsa On2 and never looked back.

Initially teaching for Salsa Belfast, Erin went on to join forces with Suzanne when they started their own company 'Salsa Style' in 2013, bringing international teachers to Belfast for both weekly classes and monthly parties. From this they taught with Bachata Fever and trained the student choreo groups performing at The Scottish Salsa Congress and Mambo City 5*Star Congress in London. Along side this, Erin joined the top ladies styling group in Dublin directed by Georgia Timmins. This resulted in performances at several UK and European festivals including Riga Salsa Festival.

In 2016, after moving to London, Erin was asked to guest teach for Incognito and was part of the Caramelo teachers programme. Her love for performing lived on and she joined the Karel Flores Choreography group under the direction of Shelley from Pexava.

Taking a break from teaching, Erin knew the value in continuing her salsa journey through taking as many classes and trainings as possible, this included with TNT were she was often asked to demo the workshops and ladies styling with Kat Gosling (Kat and Eddie).

Most recently, she has been taking part in online training games and musicality warm-up's with her mentor Svetlana Ray who has completely transformed her dancing through technique, musicality, unlimited footwork all within a fun, inspiring and supportive international community.
